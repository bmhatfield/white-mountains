YMCA Alpine Club 100 Highest List

From the YMCA Alpine Club list:

The Alpine Club has created the NH 100 highest list, containing 100 highest peaks in NH that have a hiking trail to the summit. Out hope is that this list will inspire you to visit some new "wild" places and attain new goals. The list was last updated January 2005, utilizing the AMC White Mountain Guide, AMC Southern NH Trail Guide and the Cohos Trail Guide. The list has and will undoubtedly go through future revisions.
