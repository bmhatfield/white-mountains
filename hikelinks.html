New England Hiking Sites

Hike the Whites! Welcome to the White Mountains of New Hampshire! This site is dedicated to hiking, backpacking, skiing, snowshoeing, and all around enjoyment of the outdoors in this wonderful place. An outstanding site that I strongly recommend.

AlpineZone.Com, Skiing, hiking and more in New England.

Chicoine Family Journal -- New England Hiking Journal: experiences on the trails of the White Mountains.

Get Outdoors, New England is an Internet-based bulletin board for outdoor activities in and around the New England area---and sometimes beyond!

The Great Outdoors, an indoor site for outdoor dreaming.

Guide to New Hampshire Trails. Where to hike in NH. New Hampshire Mountains, Trails, Fall Foliage, Cross Country. Submit Your Experience.

Hike New Hampshire. Your source for hiking and backpacking information in the White Mountains of New Hampshire.

Hiking Trails on Mt. Monadnock. This site began as a guide for new hikers of the mountain who had won a charity auction for a guided tour to the summit. It focused on the trails from the main part of Monadnock State Park to the summit. Since then, the site has been slowly including other trails, and more will follow.

The Leisurely Backpacker. I'd like to think that a Leisurely Backpacker is someone who is on the trail for the trail itself, and not the destination. Something to do with that whole "each step is a journey in itself" sort of philosophy, eh?

MountWashington.Com

Welcome to New Hampshire Outdoors, we have the concept of providing a resource for Outdoor activities around the state. This site was created with the desire to collect information and share it with our fellow NH residents.

New Hampshire Hikes and Walks. A guide to places to hike and walk in New Hampshire, with comments allowed.

NH Trails - New Hampshire's Hiking Trail Guide. A Guide To New Hampshire's Hiking Trails: submit your pictures, stories, comments and questions for others to see.

Outdoors New England . A company in New London, Connecticut unabashedly promoting the people, communities, organizations and independent businesses of the real New England.

White Mountain Backpacking. One woman's quest to conquer the 48 4,000 footers.

Other Hiking Sites

Adirondack High Peaks Region by Jacques et Johanne. Welcome to the outdoor web page of "Jacques et Johanne". This site is dedicated to hiking in the Adirondack region. You will find here pictures of the 47 peaks above 4000 feet, our suggestion of trails and some planning tips.

Adirondack Journey, designed for Adirondack hikers by Adirondack hikers. Hundreds of photos of and from the Adirondack High Peaks with trail descriptions. Cross-referenced to the most popular guidebooks.

Birdhead Studios. Serious Mountain Humor! Videos from the 46 ADK High Peaks, Pics from the Northeastern 4000 footers, cartoons, Richard Bern posts, Nip Nip-The Evil Bunny, the home of the best ADK Site award and much, much more!

RANDONNÉE AVENTURE offers trips to scenic areas in Quebec, New England, and New York State.

Backpacker.com -- The Website of Wilderness Travel.

Hiking and Backpacking - Hiking and Backpacking.com Hiking and backpacking info and where to find the best deals on outdoor gear.

Slackpacker A guide to trails and whatnot for the lazy backpacker...the slackpacker.

Montana Fly Fishing & Hiking Information at Big Sky Fishing.Com. A guide to fly fishing in Montana. Includes hiking information and extensive photographs of Glacier National Park and many of the other mountain ranges in Montana.
