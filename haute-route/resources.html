Resources for the Chamonix - Zermatt Haute Route

I have collected quite a bit of potentially useful information, mostly in English but some in French (I do not read German), which I have divided up as follows:

  • General Information including visa information, tourist offices, transport information, hiking clubs, weather services, and a couple of sites that did not fit elsewhere.

  • Maps that are needed for an Haute Route traverse, plus many that are not "needed" but may be of interest to map lovers.

  • Books. Yes, there is more to the Haute Route than Kev Reynolds' guide!!!

  • Haute Route Trip Reports that I have found. There must be more, but Google searches on most Haute Route terms are not very rewarding.

  • Lodging. Here I usually give pointers to tourist offices, rather than to individual establishments, since the local tourist office can track changes better than I can!

  • Huts. Mainly pointers to the individual hut web sites, plus a few comments on huts in general.
