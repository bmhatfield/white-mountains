Hiker Accidents and Deaths in New Hampshire

Rather than attempt to keep an up to date archive of stories on hiking accidents in the White Mountains of New Hampshire I will give a set of resources that should help you find information as soon as it is available on the net.

  • An excellent source of information is Google News, here is a link to a search for hiker accident OR death "new hampshire" OR "white mountains".

    You can also get news alerts from Google News, a mechanism that some people find very useful.

  • The New Hampshire Fish and Game Department newsroom is the source of authoritative reports.

  • New Hampshire media sources include WMUR news, the Concord Monitor and the Union Leader Outdoors section.

  • Sometimes news of accidents in the White Mountains first shows up on the boston.com news section.

  • Once the news is on the net is is quickly discussed on the major New England hiking bulletin boards: AMC BBs, the Views from the Top forums and the AlpineZone forums.

  • A good way of avoiding being the victim of an accident is to carefully study the web site of the hikeSafe Initiative, a joint initiative of the New Hampshire Fish and Game Department and of the White Mountain National Forest.
